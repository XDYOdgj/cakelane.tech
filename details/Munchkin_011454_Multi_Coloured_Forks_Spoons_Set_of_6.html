Munchkin Cutlery Set - Multi-Coloured Forks and Spoons for Independent Eating

As your little one takes their first steps toward self-sufficiency, the Munchkin 011454 Cutlery Set becomes an essential companion. This set of 6 includes 3 curved, rounded forks and 3 deep spoons, thoughtfully crafted for babies and toddlers learning to feed themselves safely and confidently.

Designed with child development in mind, the curved shape of the forks makes it easier for small hands to grip and transition to regular cutlery. These versatile utensils are perfect for use at home, daycare, or during special moments with grandparents, making feeding a joyful and interactive experience.
